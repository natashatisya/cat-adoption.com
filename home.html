Cat only accommodations

Your cat stays in one of our custom built kitty suites (no cages) designed to keep cats happy and secure while away from home!

Any special diet (including Rx food) that your cat is currently eating would need to continue during their stay with us.We have developed a successful process of introducing cats to a new group of kitties without stress or anxiety.

Short & long term stays

We offer short and long term cat boarding accommodations for cats of all ages!

The longer your kitty stays with us the lower the cost per day.

Medical & special needs

Our trained staff can easily manage medical and special needs kitties! We even offer post surgical and isolation boarding!
